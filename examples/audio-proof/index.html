URK Audio Flow

DOM-first audio transport proof for staged loading, explicit playback state, pointer and keyboard control, and lifecycle-safe pause, resume, and shutdown.

Keyboard

  • 1, 2, and 3 start the three proof tracks.
  • Space toggles play and pause on the active track.
  • M toggles mute and S stops the active track.
  • ArrowUp and ArrowDown adjust volume in 10% steps.
